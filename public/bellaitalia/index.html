Our Story

A Family Tradition Since 1985

What started as a small trattoria in Naples has blossomed into a beloved Singapore destination. Our family's passion for authentic Italian cuisine drives everything we do.

Every dish is crafted with imported Italian ingredients, traditional techniques, and a whole lot of love. From our hand-rolled pasta to our wood-fired pizzas, taste the difference of true Italian cooking.

- Chef Marco Rossi
